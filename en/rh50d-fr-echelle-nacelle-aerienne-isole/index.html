RH50D Insulated

Technical Specifications

Working Height (A) 15.24m (50')
Horizontal Reach (B) 11.88m (38'11")
Retracted Length (C) 5.78m (18'11")
Interior Width (D) 49 cm (19")
Bucket Capacity 159 kg (350 lbs)
Lifting Ring 250 kg (550 lbs)
Rotation 360° Cont.
Elevation -5° à 80°
Approx. Weight 658 kg
Min. GVWR 8620 kg (19000 lbs)
Hydraulic Pressure 20684 kPa (3000 psi)

The RH50D is an insulated telescoping ladder with a working height of 50 ft. (15.24 m), comprising three ladder sections for optimal effectiveness. Ideal for work requiring a longer reach (e.g., utility poles offset far from the road) since the side reach can take you all the way to 39 ft. (12 m). The lift ring located at the end of the second section is a standard accessory and can lift loads of 550 lbs (250 kg) in any position, regardless of the chosen vehicle.

This unit can be installed on different chassis as long as the GVWR (gross vehicle weight rating) is greater than 19, 000 lbs (8,620 kg) like Hino, Mitsubishi, International, Freightliner and so many more. And that’s not to mention the many other benefits and options available to ensure that RH aerial ladder equipment meets your precise needs.

Key in hand option is available. Please contact us to see what we can do for your preferred chassis and configuration.

In addition, we can provide additional security by adding a system such as Ozone-tech. This product requires removing the keys from the ignition; press an activator button to operate the unit. Ozone-tech is perfect for fleet owners that want to have some control over the fuel consumption. The system ensures optimum battery management and will turn on the engine if the vehicle needs to recharge the battery to its maximum capacity.

Accuracy is crucial to achieve consistent quality. RH Ladder uses water jet and laser cutting, along with robotic welding, to deliver constant precision and reliability.

Important Note

This type of aerial ladder must be installed on vehicles whose GVWR (gross vehicle weight rating) is greater than 9,500 lbs (4,309 kg). Outriggers are generally not required. However, given the variety of configurations possible, outrigger requirements must be confirmed by means of stability tests performed on your bucket truck.

The same applies to the bucket’s load-carrying capacity (in other words, the maximum weight authorized to be carried in the bucket). Specifically, this telescoping ladder is rated to carry 350 lbs (159 kg); however, its actual load-carrying capacity depends on the carrier vehicle.

We encourage you to contact Robert Hydraulique to obtain more information on the restrictions and factors to be taken into account when installing RH telescoping aerial ladders so that you can meet the highest standards in quality and safety.

*** Always ensure that you are complying with all local regulations when installing bucket-equipped aerial ladders. Robert Hydraulique is not responsible for installations performed outside its production facilities.

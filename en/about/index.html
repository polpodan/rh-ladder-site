Over 35 Years of Innovation

The Origin of Success

Founded in 1991 by the visionary team at Robert Hydraulique, the company set out with a clear mission: to redefine aerial ladder industry standards. What began as a specialized workshop has evolved over three decades into a global leader recognized for precision engineering.

Based in Lanoraie, Quebec, we design, manufacture, and assemble units that travel across North America and beyond. Our strength lies in our ability to listen to technicians in the field to provide them with the ultimate work tool.

The Elite Era (2024-2026)

The future of RH Equipment is being written today with the launch of the Elite Division (RHED) . This new branch represents the peak of our expertise, introducing 100% electric units capable of operating in the most hostile environments with unparalleled silence and cleanliness.

By integrating STRENX® high-performance steel, we have achieved the feat of reducing structural weight by 40% while increasing durability. Coupled with our exclusive Ozone-tech technology, we offer eco-responsible energy management that allows working without leaving the truck's engine running.

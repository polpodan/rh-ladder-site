Pre-purchase service

Are you considering getting second-hand HR equipment?

Your safety is important to us, and we want to guarantee the quality of our product.
No worries, you can bring it to us, our team of professional technicians will be happy to do an evaluation of the equipment.

The evaluation of the equipment will allow you to know:

+ The age of the vehicle
+ The general condition
+ The remaining life of the vehicle
+ The condition of the main components such as battery, motor, transmission
+ The residual value of the equipment It is through this evaluation that you will know for sure about the reliability of the second hand unit you are considering.
